## 🚀 How It Works

### **Article Fetching Process**
```
Every 24 hours at midnight UTC
    ↓
Hacker News API (top 150 stories)
    ↓
Filter by 40+ keywords (AI, business, tech, funding, etc.)
    ↓
Fetch individual story details
    ↓
Extract: title, description, category, source, URL, publish time
    ↓
Auto-categorize into 7 categories
    ↓
Insert into SQLite database
    ↓
Remove duplicates
    ↓
Up to 100 unique articles added
```
